## About me

I am a 4th year PhD student in Economics at Stanford GSB. I am interested in mechanism design, in particular multidimensional screening and redesigning social programs. I have recently been working on mechanisms allocating goods through waitlists. You can reach me at tokarski (at) stanford [dot] edu. My CV is [here](https://ftokarski.github.io/CV/FTCV.pdf).
